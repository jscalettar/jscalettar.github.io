THE GRAVITY OF THE SITUTATION

Programmer and Artist

GameMaker Platformer (Mar.-June 2014)

In this platformer, you play as a squirrel who is struggling to escape the pit she was pulled into. The player will need to utilize the unique mechanic of throwing an acorn onto appropriate surfaces which generates a black hole, where there is a stronger the pull toward its center the closer the player is to its center, in order to traverse the map and jump to platforms they otherwise couldn't jump to.
