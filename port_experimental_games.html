EXPERIMENTAL GAMES

Created Solo

Here are a few smaller projects I have worked on by myself:

Surroundings

Interactive Twine narrative (Jan. 2017)

A short text-based adventure game where you begin as a person just getting off work with all thoughts on going to buy a new video game and then play it all night.

  • Play Surroundings


Variable Lovecraft

Interactive Twine narrative (Oct. 2015)

An adaptation of The Cats of Ulthar by H.P. Lovecraft where every pass through the story yields a new variation.

  • Play Variable Lovecraft


The Wolf Roams

Interactive Twine narrative (Oct. 2015)

An interactive narrative based off the Aesop's Fable The Wolf and the Lion. In The Wolf Roams, You play as a wolf who discovers different paths and thinks differently depending on your choices. Your decisions will alter the outcome of the Wolf’s night and the moral of the story.

  • Play The Wolf Roams


Memory Snake

JavaScript Game (Jan. 2015)

A variation on the tradition snake game that adds difficulty by making the pellets all turn invisible after a certain amount of time. In addition to this new mechanic, there are also now 4 types of pellets:

Normal: size+1 and score+1
Rotten: size-1 and score-1
Ripe: size+0 and score+5
Reveal: reveals normal and rotten food

Attempt to get as many points as possible before colliding with a wall or your own body!

  • Play Memory Snake
