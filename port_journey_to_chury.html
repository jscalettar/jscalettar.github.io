JOURNEY TO CHURY

Lead Programmer on Gameplay

JavaScript Game (Jan.-Mar. 2015)

A JavaScript game based on the story of the European Space Association (ESA) launching the Rosetta Orbiter in 2004 which successfully launched a probe that landed on the comet, Chury, in this game you play as the Rosetta Satellite on its journey to Chury. The novel aspect of this game is the mechanic of having to rotate an elongated sprite to dodge space debris as well as moving around the screen. Along the way, you can collect minerals to increase your score. Once you reach Chury, you will then have to launch and land your probe on the comet.
